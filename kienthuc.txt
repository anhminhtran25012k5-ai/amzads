KIEN THUC BULKSHEETS 2.0 - SPONSORED PRODUCTS

1. DINH DANG DU LIEU CO BAN
- Ngay thang: Bat buoc yyyyMMdd (VD: 20240405).
- Tien te (Bid/Budget): Chi dung so, khong dung ky hieu (VD: 0.75). Dung dau cham thap phan.
- Phan tram: Dung so nguyen (VD: 35 thay vi 35%).

2. TRUONG OPERATION (HANH DONG)
- create: Tao thuc the moi. Dung ten tam (text) trong cac cot ID de lien ket me-con.
- update: Chinh sua thuc the da co. Bat buoc dung ID so (numeric ID) tu he thong.
- archive: Xoa vinh vien (khong the khoi phuc).
- De trong: He thong bo qua dong do.

3. CAU TRUC PHAN CAP (HIERARCHY)
- Cap 1: Portfolio (Tuy chon)
- Cap 2: Campaign
- Cap 3: Ad Group | Bidding Adjustment
- Cap 4: Keyword | Product Ad (SKU) | Product Targeting | Negative Keyword

4. CAC COT BAT BUOC (CREATE)
- Campaign: Product, Entity, Operation, Campaign ID (ten tam), Campaign Name, Start Date, Targeting Type (manual/auto), State, Daily Budget.
- Ad Group: Product, Entity, Operation, Campaign ID, Ad Group ID (ten tam), Ad Group Name, Ad Group Default Bid, State.
- Keyword: Product, Entity, Operation, Campaign ID, Ad Group ID, Keyword Text, Match Type, Bid, State.
- Product Ad: Product, Entity, Operation, Campaign ID, Ad Group ID, SKU, State.

5. CAC COT BAT BUOC (UPDATE)
- Moi thuc the: Product, Entity, Operation.
- Dinh danh: Phai co ID so (Campaign ID, Ad Group ID, Keyword ID,...) cua thuc the can sua.
- Chinh sua: Chi dien gia tri moi vao cac cot can thay doi (VD: Bid, State, Budget). Cac cot khac co the de trong.

6. LUU Y QUAN TRONG
- Thu tu dong: Me phai nam tren con (Campaign > Ad Group > Keyword).
- Keyword Match Type: exact, phrase, broad.
- State: enabled, paused, archived.
- Targeting Type: manual, auto.
- Ad Group Default Bid: Bat buoc khi tao Ad Group.
- Keyword Bid: Neu de trong se lay theo Bid mac dinh cua Ad Group.
